Volunteers for Hospitalized Veterans

Mission Statement

To serve veterans at the Rocky Mountain Regional VA Medical Center and other units of the VA Eastern Colorado Health Care System in order to provide for the well being as well as financial needs of veterans. In addition we will support units and programs for the homeless veteran population. A Donation to Volunteers of Hospitalized Veterans helps veterans!

Volunteers for Hospitalized Veterans, Inc. (V4HV) is honored to have served veterans of the Colorado VA since 1997.

Primary charitable activities
• Supply clothing for the inpatient mental health unit to benefit homeless veterans
• Host special events and support recreation therapy programs
• Finance specific needs of veterans facing hardships as identified and requested by VA staff

Who we are
V4HV directors include members of various VAVS Committee organizations including The Sons of The American Legion, the Marine Corps League, The American Legion Auxiliary, the Marine Corps League Auxiliary as well as VA volunteers.

Come see us at the Rocky Mountain Regional VA Med Center popcorn stand on Wednesdays!
